Expert care for infants, children, and adolescents in a warm and safe environment.

Dedicated Child-Focused Care

Our paediatric department provides high-quality, compassionate healthcare for children from birth to adolescence. We combine medical excellence with child-friendly care, ensuring your little ones grow up healthy and strong.

  • Childhood immunizations and regular checkups
  • Treatment of common paediatric conditions
  • Growth and developmental assessments
  • Nutrition counseling for infants and children
  • Emergency and specialist referrals when needed
